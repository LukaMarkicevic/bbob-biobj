Welcome to the supplementary material web page for the bbob-biobj and bbob-biobj-ext test suites in COCO.


This will become the supplementary material web page, providing visualizations of all 55 respectively 92 functions of the bbob-biobj and bbob-biobj-ext test suites of the Comparing Continous Optimizers platform, as introduced in the paper

D. Brockhoff, T. Tušar, A. Auger, and N. Hansen. Using Well-Understood Single-Objective Functions in Multiobjective Black-Box Optimization Test Suites. Submitted to Evolutionary Computation Journal.

Before all plots will be available here, we refer to the previously produced plots, provided on the soon deprecated bbob-biobj GForge webpage.
